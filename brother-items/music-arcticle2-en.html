“Xiao Qiu Yue Ming”:
Enjoying the Moon and Drinking the Wine

Author: Tea and Wine


    When you mention Guofeng music, what is your first thought? Is the river map, the silver side of these long-standing ancient wind circle, or popular electronic song Ji Luo Tianyi? Xiao Bian knows that these great national wind music works are well known, so Xiao Bian today brought you a relatively small song: "Xiao Qiu Yue Ming".
     It is worth mentioning that this song is composed by the songwriter Solpie using the Japanese electronic singer Jiuyin's future sound library. The whole song is sung by Japanese voice source to imitate the Chinese pronunciation. Maybe during the listening process. I feel that the Chinese pronunciation is slightly uncomfortable, just like listening to the Japanese singing Chinese songs, but this does not affect the beauty of Xiaoqiu Yueming, but it has the most unique taste, and it is impossible to have any version of the cover. The feeling of imitation.
    At the beginning of the song is a dulcimer and drums. The song is opened with some lively and naughty melodies. Then the hole is added under the dulcimer and the drums. It seems that the autumn night is slowly spread: the night is just right, the moon is bright. High hanging, autumn wind is cool, boasting the laurel tree, the girl dressed in plain clothes sits at the stone table in the courtyard and enjoys her own discretion, but appreciates the bright moon in front of her eyes but remembers the past.
    he guzheng in the accompaniment of the main song uses techniques such as fingerwheels, arpeggios and sweeping strings to make the songs smooth and agile, especially the section of the second section of the main song and the chorus transition, which makes the whole mood open. stand up.
    The style of "Xiao Qiu Yue Ming" is very obvious in the chorus part. Almost every sentence is sung with a long sound, which makes the tail sound like a small hook. I have to say that the original version will be so smooth and natural, it can be said that it is comparable to a real person. Of course, Xiao Bian also listened to the cover version, and the singer who is good at handling the transfer is really awkward.
     This song depicts the picture of Mid-Autumn Festival Moonlight Night. As a traditional Chinese festival, it is also a frequent visitor to the national culture. In fact, Guofeng culture is not a thing of great spring and snow, it is around us, just like the Mid-Autumn Festival that everyone can pass. National wind belongs to every national. Xiao Bian once saw such a sentence, "Understanding traditional Chinese characters should be the passive skills of the Chinese people." Today, with the rapid development of all aspects, traditional culture is rapidly losing, and the formation of national culture is a good phenomenon. Therefore, Xiaobian also hopes that more and more people will pay attention to and support the national culture and make it grow stronger.
