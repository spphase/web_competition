Lizhi Zhang: Ancient Dwellings have
revived the cultural memory of Hutou


Source: Anxi News Author: Zhenhui Li



        The newspaper "has the privilege of witnessing the Annan Lake heads in Minnan, and the mood is like the sudden encounter of old friends who have not seen for many years!" On the 22nd, Zhang Zhizhi, assistant researcher of Tsinghua University School of Architecture, visited the new town and old town of Hutou Town. When the ancient dwellings and ancient buildings of the Ming and Qing Dynasties were sighed, "the maintenance of old houses is very difficult. The ancient dwelling buildings with at least five state-level cultural protection units in a town are quite rare in the country. They have revived the town of Hutou. Cultural memory."

The "water" of the ancient folk culture of Hutou is very deep


        "Like the pillars of the Xinyi Hall, the two drums in the middle of the drum have the typical characteristics of the ancient buildings in the north. This style is very rare after the Ming and Qing Dynasties." Zhang Zhizhi introduced this series of Ming and Qing buildings. Axis symmetrical arrangement and multi-level depth, front and rear organic connection to layout, interior of the building, up and down, drop, patio, wing room, hall, Guardian, hanging mountain curve dovetail, hard mountain roof or horsehead gable It is all-inclusive, showing the folk palace-style architectural style, ancient architectural culture and typical traditional residences in southern Anhui during the Ming and Qing Dynasties.
        “The old houses in many places have their own side to show off their wealth, but the culture of these ancient houses in Hutou Town feels very good!” In the view of Zhang Zhizhi, these ancient dwellings in Hutou Town are large and concentrated, with orderly planning and construction. The specifications are high, and there is an elite temperament, which is much deeper than the general residential culture. Intuitively, the engraving and decoration are less, the color is darker and heavier than jumping, the whole building layout is Shulang, and the aesthetics are created with more elegant curves, space and color rhythm. This kind of architecture seeks simplicity, not skill, and aesthetic taste is higher, which reflects the characteristics of the ancient aesthetic control.
        Hutou Town is a mirror of the social history of the early Qing Dynasty. Nowadays, ancient dwellings in many places are after Qianlong. In the early Qing Dynasty, it was not only the low tide of Chinese construction, but also the low tide of the construction of the world, like the new buildings and the old buildings. The group is actually very few. At the same time, you can see the cultural traces left by the top figures of the time, such as Kangxi, Li Guangdi, Wang Shimin, Zhang Bolong, etc. The cultural 'water' is very deep. These buildings are called 'living fossils' of the ancient Shunan." Zhang Zhizhi said.

Repairing ancient buildings requires "minimum intervention"


        Ancient architecture is the carrier of historical culture and the soul of urban memory. Protecting it is to protect the spiritual wealth left by our nation and ancestors. In the process of urbanization, how to protect historical and cultural heritage is a problem that cannot be bypassed.
        “The renovation of the ancient residential buildings in Hutou should be done in the early stage. The architecture of each era is different. It is impossible to repair all the ancient urns according to a kind of “Southern architectural style”, but this is a common phenomenon today. Therefore, it is necessary to conduct in-depth research on the ancient houses of Hutou, because they are too important, and if they are really repaired, they will be lost."
        "Wherever the buildings listed in the national key cultural relics protection units are repaired, the originality and integrity of the cultural relics must be paid attention to. There must be a 'minimum intervention', 'seeking the old and not seeking new' consciousness, an old thing is worth more than a thousand new things." Zhang Zhizhi introduced that in Japan, the repair of this kind of house will last for a long time. A thousand-page repair report will be formed, which will record the characteristics and damage of all components, and then interfere with the building as little as possible. The components that have to be replaced will be specially saved, etc. In short, it is saved as much as possible. Repair the previous information.
        "The Ming and Qing architectural complexes in Hutou Town are our common spiritual homeland, bearing the soul of our national culture and retaining our homesickness. Only by caring for these historical and cultural resources, Hutou is a character and soul. A historical and cultural town with feelings!" said Zhang Zhizhi.
