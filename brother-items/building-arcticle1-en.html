Do you really understand the new Chinese architecture?

resource:sohu



  A city in the old country of the East, in the construction, if you completely lose your artistic identity, it is painful in terms of cultural expression and perspective. Because this fact clearly represents the decline of our culture, as it is eliminated. As the architect Liang Sicheng said, for a long time, our vision was filled with buildings named after the exotic, Tuscany, Mediterranean, American style, French romance... once became the fashion of China's real estate market. . Fortunately, with China's comprehensive national strength, international status and influence, and China's strategy of strengthening cultural self-confidence and cultural power, the "Chinese style" has now become the mainstream of the world. The "new Chinese architecture" with Chinese characteristics has gradually been accepted and even sought after by everyone, and it has been popular all over the country.
However, when you are enamored with it, do you really understand or understand what is a new Chinese architecture?


  • Northern Style

  • Southern Style

  • New Style

  • Northern Style – Splendid


      Fangxiang, courtyard and door are the core of this new Chinese architecture. High wall enclosing and paying attention to the sense of order, whether it is architectural planning or garden construction, it is mainly borrowed from the royal family. The materials are mostly gray brick and white marble, and the ancient palaces and palace elements such as the drum stone, the palace lantern and the plaque are introduced to form the majestic. Grand momentum.


  • Southern Style – Elegant


      White-walled blue tiles, horse-head walls and cornices are prominent features of this new Chinese-style building. The courtyard setting mainly highlights the small bridges flowing in the Jiangnan gardens. The overall image is like Chinese ink painting, which is light and elegant, and has a poetic beauty. Of course, the two can also be mutually interconnected. For example, the Northern School can learn from the southern gardening techniques in the garden, and the Southern School can also draw the representative elements of the northern architecture.


  • New Style – Special


      From the literal point of view, the two can not be completely equal, but it is also a bit related. As we all know, Asia contains countries with a long history such as China, India, North Korea, South Korea and Japan. The cultures of Asian countries have certain similarities. This is because they are in Han, Tang, Ming and Qing. In the dynasty, Chinese culture has been in constant cultural integration with many Asian countries including Japan, South Korea, Thailand and Indonesia. It is worth noting that in modern times, regardless of geographical location, politics, economy, culture, etc., Asian culture also refers to oriental culture, so some new Chinese projects claim to be "Oriental", which is one-sided, and "new Asia is considered to be a "new Chinese style", which is even more a joke. In short, the new Asian architectural style pays attention to the flowing lines, the relaxing decoration, the retro Chinese style hollowing out... and pays attention to the “inner” spiritual realm (Zen culture) pursued by the oriental culture. shape. Sometimes it will be integrated into Western culture. The multi-element is inclusive, multi-handed and mixed, creating a new lifestyle that is compatible with classics and fashion, and the perfect combination of art and elegance, which is more in line with the tastes of contemporary people.
